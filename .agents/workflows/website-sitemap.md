Always create the website following the sitemap below:

**HOMEPAGE:**

- Hero Section: Headline, subheadline, CTA. Image background

- Services Section: Boxes with photo, service name, short description (max. 15 words)

- About Us Section: 2 columns, one with title and text (around 150 words), and a button with link to About Us Page. The other column with an image, or 2 images overlaping each other

- Highlight Numbers: information such as: x years in business/experience, x clients served, X% customer satisfaction, etc. (maximum of 4 numbers)

Featured Projects: title, and a grid with Photo, City

Testimonials: Title, carroussel with testimonial boxes containing testimonial text, client name, client city

Final CTA: Headline, subheadline, CTA

Footer: column with links to website pages, column with social media links


**ABOUT US PAGE:**

- Hero section: headline, subheadline, background image

- Our story: e columns section, one with text about their story (maximum of 200 words). the other one with an image.

- Our values: Mission, Vision, values

- Team: Photo, name, Title of each team member


**SERVICES PAGE:**

- Hero section: headline, subheadline, background image

- Services section: Boxes with photo, service name, short description (max. 15 words). Repeat the services section from the homepage here.

- Featured Projects: Photo, City (repeat the Featured Projects section from the homepage here)

- Testimonials: Testimonial text, client name, client city (repeat the testimonials section from the homepage here)

- Final CTA: Headline, subheadline, CTA


**CONTACT PAGE:**

- Hero section: headline, subheadline, background image

- 2 column section, one with contact info: email, phone number, address, social media. The other one with the contact Form

- Section with maps pointing to the business address.
