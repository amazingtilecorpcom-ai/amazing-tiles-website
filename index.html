The Amazing Tile Corp

Craftsmanship, Integrity, and Respect.

Amazing Tile Corp is a family-owned tile installation company serving Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Maine. Built on craftsmanship, integrity, and respect, our company has earned a strong reputation through word-of-mouth and long-term partnerships with homeowners, designers, and contractors.

Founder Jaison Borba began his career in construction as a master builder and brought that same level of precision and discipline to tile installation. Today, Amazing Tile Corp is known for delivering exceptional quality, maintaining clean and organized job sites, and completing projects ahead of schedule without compromising standards.
